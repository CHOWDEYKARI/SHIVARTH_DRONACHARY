Restaurants

  • 1. Paradise Biryani: Paradise Biryani is an iconic restaurant in Hyderabad, renowned for its flavorful and aromatic biryanis. With multiple branches across the city, it has been serving delectable biryanis for several decades.
  • 2. Chutneys: Chutneys is a popular vegetarian restaurant with branches in Banjara Hills and Madhapur. It is known for its extensive South Indian and North Indian buffet, featuring a variety of chutneys and traditional dishes.
  • 3. Dakshin: Located in the ITC Kakatiya Hotel, Dakshin is a fine-dining restaurant that specializes in authentic South Indian cuisine. It offers a wide array of dishes from different states of South India, showcasing the rich flavors and culinary traditions of the region.
  • 4. Ohri's Jiva Imperia: Situated in Madhapur, Ohri's Jiva Imperia is a luxurious restaurant that serves a fusion of Indian and international cuisines. The elegant ambiance, attentive service, and delectable menu make it a favorite dining spot for special occasions.
  • 5. AB's - Absolute Barbecues: AB's is a popular barbecue buffet restaurant with branches in Jubilee Hills and Gachibowli. It offers a unique dining experience, where customers can indulge in unlimited grills and a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
  • 6. Olive Bistro: Olive Bistro, located in Jubilee Hills, is a charming restaurant known for its Mediterranean-inspired cuisine. The restaurant offers a delightful ambiance, a picturesque outdoor seating area, and a menu featuring an array of salads, pastas, pizzas, and more.
