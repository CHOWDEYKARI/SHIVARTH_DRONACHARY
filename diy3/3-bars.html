Bars

  • 1. Over The Moon - Located in Jubilee Hills, this rooftop bar offers a chic and elegant setting. It features a wide range of cocktails, spirits, and a diverse food menu, along with live music.
  • 2. The Sky Kitchen - Situated in Gachibowli, this rooftop bar provides a stunning panoramic view of the city. It offers an extensive selection of cocktails, along with a fusion menu.
  • 3. Kismet - Found in Banjara Hills, this upscale bar and lounge is known for its classy ambiance and excellent drinks. It offers an extensive collection of spirits, signature cocktails, and a lively atmosphere.
  • 4. Karma Lounge - Nestled in Hitech City, this bar offers a relaxed and cozy setting. It serves a variety of drinks, including innovative cocktails, along with a range of appetizers.
